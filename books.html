Book List

A personal selection of books I recommend.

The White Masai

Corinne Hofmann

A crazy story with a wild narrative rhythm.

Guns, Germs, and Steel: The Fates of Human Societies

Jared Diamond

Some theories turned out to be wrong, but it is still a great book.

Mare Aperto

Luca Misculin

A compelling history of the Mediterranean.

L'Anniversario (The Anniversary)

Andrea Bajani

Quite deep despite the apparently unimpressive plot.

L'Amica Geniale (My Brilliant Friend)

Elena Ferrante

Across four volumes, it tells one of the most intriguing relationships ever written.

Harry Potter

J. K. Rowling

A classic.

King Solomon's Ring

Konrad Lorenz

If you want to learn about ethology, this is your bible.

Le Otto Montagne (The Eight Mountains)

Paolo Cognetti

A special story about friendship.

Vita mia (My Life)

Dacia Maraini

Real memories of a girl in a Japanese prison camp during WW2.

The Royal Game

Stefan Zweig

A short novel about chess that I finished during a six-hour flight.

The Three-Body Problem

Cixin Liu

A trilogy that shows how sci-fi books should be written.

Sovietistan

Erika Fatland

An odyssey through Central Asia.
