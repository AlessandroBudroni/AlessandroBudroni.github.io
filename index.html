About me

I received the B.S. degree in Pure Mathematics from the University of Cagliari and the M.Sc. degree in Coding Theory and Cryptography from the University of Trento. I got my PhD degree in Computer Security at the University of Bergen, Norway, under the supervision of Professor Igor Semaev. Before my PhD, I worked at MIRACL Ltd in London for one year as a Cryptography Engineer. Since February 2022, I have been working at the Cryptography Research Center of the Technology Innovation Institute in Abu Dhabi.

Hobbies: wildlife photography, outdoor sports (skiing, hiking, surfing, …). Also, I have been a semi-professional juggler and I performed in international juggling conventions and festivals (I also wrote a blog post about Math and Juggling).

Some public repositories that might be useful

  • A Python3 implementation of PERK v2.2.0 that mirrors the official PERK C reference implementation.

  • You can find here a library that benchmarks and compares different algorithms for sampling random permutations.

  • I am the creator of the rbbl (RAM-Based BKW for LWE) library: a fast and parallelized library written in C to solve LWE instances.

  • I am one of the creators of the fbbl (File-Based BKW for LWE) library. This is the first implementation of the BKW algorithm for LWE able to solve relatively large LWE instances.

  • In the past, I have been mantaining the Apache milagro-crypto-c and milagro-crypto-js libraries.
