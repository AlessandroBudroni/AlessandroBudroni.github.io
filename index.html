About me

I received the B.S. degree at University of Cagliari in pure Mathematics and the M.Sc. degree at University of Trento in Coding Theory and Cryptography. I am now a PhD candidate in Computer Security at Univeristy of Bergen. I recently joined the Cryptography Research Center at the Technology Innovation Institute in Abu Dhabi.

I am always interested in hearing from colleagues, other cryptography fans, or just interesting creative folk, so feel free to contact me.

Hobbies: skiing (downhill and cross-country), surfing, hiking, climbing, coding, juggling.

Coding

  • EHT-C is my PoC of our new public-key cryptosystem EHT on github.

  • I am the creator of the rbbl (RAM-Based BKW for LWE) library: a fast and parallelized library written in C to solve LWE instances.

  • I mantain the fbbl (File-Based BKW for LWE) library. This is the first implementation of the BKW algorithm for LWE able to solve relatively large LWE instances.

  • I have been mantaining the Apache milagro-crypto-c and milagro-crypto-js libraries, IMO the best libraries in c and javascript supporoting pairing-based cryptography! Now I follow the projects and I plan to be more active again in the future.
